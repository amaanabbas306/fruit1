Apple



The apple is a popular and nutritious fruit known for its crisp texture and sweet flavor. It comes in a variety of colors, including red, green, and yellow, and is rich in dietary fiber, vitamin C.

Mango

Mango, often referred to as the "king of fruits," is a tropical fruit known for its sweet and juicy flesh. It has a distinctive flavor and comes in various varieties, each with its own unique taste and texture.
